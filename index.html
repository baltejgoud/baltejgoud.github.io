AI-Based Deep Fake Detection

A deep learning system that distinguishes AI-generated fake videos from real ones, tackling deepfakes used in political distress, fraud, and misinformation. Uses AI to fight AI.

How it works: A ResNeXt CNN extracts frame-level features, which are then passed to an LSTM-based RNN to classify whether a video is manipulated or real. Evaluated on a mixed dataset of FaceForensics++, Deepfake Detection Challenge, and Celeb-DF.
Keywords: LSTM, RNN, FaceForensics++, Artificial Intelligence
