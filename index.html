🤖 AI-Based Deep Fake Detection

Using Artificial Intelligence to fight Artificial Intelligence

📌 Overview

Growing computation power has made creating indistinguishable human-synthesized "deepfake" videos very simple. These are used for political distress, fake terrorism events, and blackmail. This system automatically detects both replacement and reenactment deepfakes.

🧠 Feature Extraction (ResNeXt CNN)

The Res-Next Convolutional Neural Network extracts frame-level features capturing subtle facial artifacts, boundary inconsistencies, and texture anomalies that are invisible to the human eye but signal manipulation.

🗃️ Dataset

FaceForensics++ Deepfake Detection Challenge Celeb-DF

Evaluated on large amounts of balanced, mixed data to emulate real-time scenarios and improve performance on real-world data.
